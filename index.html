Mogilev

Mogilev is an ancient city in Belarus. According to the chronicle, Mogilev was founded in 1267 on the banks of the Dnieper River. The city is very cozy and beautiful. The ancient streets of the center have been restored, restored according to old photographs. There are cafes, shops, several hotels. On Leninskaya you can go to the Square of Glory. The former Gubernskaya Square is located on the city rampart. From here you have a magnificent view of the Dnieper River, the new bridge with lions, the Zadneprovye region.
